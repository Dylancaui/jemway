The Process

From discharge to care in four steps

We work directly with hospital discharge teams to make the transition from inpatient care to home support seamless and fast.

01

Hospital Refers Patient

A discharge coordinator submits a referral through our secure online form or by calling our intake line directly.

02

Needs Assessment

A Jemway care coordinator contacts the patient or family within 24 hours to understand needs, preferences, and schedule.

03

Caregiver Matched

We match the client with a trained, background-verified caregiver based on location, needs, and availability — typically within 48 hours.

04

Support Begins

Care starts at home. Clients and families track everything through the client portal. Plans are updated as needs evolve.

Who We Are

Built around what patients actually need

Jemway Solutions was founded on a simple observation: leaving the hospital is only the beginning of recovery. We exist to fill the gap between discharge and full independence at home.

Safety First

Every caregiver is background-verified, reference-checked, and trained in personal care, emergency response, and medication safety before their first placement.

Dignity in Every Visit

We select caregivers not only for competence but for character. Clients are treated with respect, patience, and genuine care throughout every interaction.

Community-Rooted

We are a Central Florida company serving Central Florida families. Our caregivers live in the same communities as our clients, and that proximity matters.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can care begin after discharge?
In most cases a caregiver is matched and scheduled within 48 hours of receiving a referral. For urgent discharges contact our intake line directly and we will prioritize placement.
How are caregivers screened and trained?
All Jemway Solutions caregivers complete a multi-step background check, professional reference verification, and our in-house training program covering personal care standards, safety protocols, medication reminders, and emergency response before any placement.
Does insurance cover home care services?
Many plans including Medicaid, Medicare Advantage, and certain private insurance programs cover qualifying home care services. We verify coverage during the intake process. Private-pay arrangements are also available.
What if a client needs a different caregiver?
We want every match to feel right. If a client or their family feels the pairing is not working, contact your care coordinator and we will arrange a new match without issue.
Where does Jemway Solutions currently operate?
We are launching in December 2027 and will initially serve Hernando, Pasco, Hillsborough, and Pinellas counties in Florida. Additional counties are planned in subsequent phases.
How do families stay informed about care?
Through the Jemway client portal, authorized family members can view the daily task log, upcoming appointments, caregiver notes, and vital sign entries in real time. Direct messaging with the assigned caregiver is also available.
